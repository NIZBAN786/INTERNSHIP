This report examines a suspicious email purporting to be from "Nationaal Thuisbatterij Centrum". It was analyzed using Sublime Security tools and manual header and content inspection. 7. Risk & Impact

This phishing attempt aims to harvest PII or lure users into fake transactions. Risks include identity theft, financial fraud, and malware infection.

8. Recommendations

  • Do not click any links or reply to the sender.
  • Report the message to IT or your mail provider.
  • Verify suspicious offers via official channels.
  • Train users to recognize social engineering cues.
